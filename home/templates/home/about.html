About

Overview

We provide a quick attendance solution in every meeting. It helps to save time and effort of the meeting organizer.

How to use

You are organizer:

  • 1. Select "New task"
  • 2. Enter Topic of Meeting, password, set time
  • 3. Confirm and copy the key that we generated
  • 4. Send the key to participants

You are participant:

  • 1. Select "Join"
  • 2. Enter the Key and press "Search"
  • 3. Click on result and enter the password
  • 4. Check to enroll to take attendance.
